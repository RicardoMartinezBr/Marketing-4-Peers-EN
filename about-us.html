Our Story

Hi my name is Ricardo, the reason I created Marketing 4 Peers was primarily because I fell in love with digital marketing/ marketing since I started my bachelor's degree in International Communications & Media in 2014.

One of the things I learned while I was still studying is that all the big companies around the world have become known because of their efforts to create a strong and well-known brand. These brands achieved this goal first through building presence on supermarket / store shelves and second through winning their place in the minds of their customers.

Knowing that the biggest difficulty that small businesses have is the financial limitations, I decided to create Marketing 4 Peers not only to sell my services but to really bring my knowledge and experience to help them reach new heights.
